Our Mission

At SERMHS Garden, we believe in the power of hands-on learning and environmental stewardship.

We're here to grow more than just plants — we're growing future environmental leaders and sustainable communities.

Through our garden program, students learn valuable skills in agriculture, science, and environmental sustainability while developing a deeper connection with nature and their community.

Our Core Values

Environmental Stewardship

We promote sustainable practices and teach students to be responsible caretakers of our environment.

Hands-on Learning

We believe in learning by doing, providing practical experience in gardening and environmental science.

Community Engagement

We foster strong connections between students, teachers, and community members through collaborative projects.

Sustainable Future

We prepare students to be advocates for environmental sustainability in their communities.
